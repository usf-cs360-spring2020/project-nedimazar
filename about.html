Why Trees, Ned?

When I stumbled upon this dataset while deciding on a project I thought it was gonna be a small set containing maybe a couple thousand trees at most. I was wrong. I think it's insane that we kept so much data on something that I thought was so simple and ordinary: trees. I grew up in Istanbul, which is a very large city but the density of trees over there is quite a bit lower so I wanted to explore this. I also wanted to pick something uplifting instead of corporate or crime data.
