Dataset

For this project, I've visualized Data SF's Street Tree List dataset.

Here's the license to the dataset by Open Data Commons

I accesed the dataset in April of 2020.

What I Kept

The original dataset contains 194K rows and 18 columns. I decided to only keep the trees which have a latitude and longitude associated with them, I also plan on doing some cleanup and grouping by species to make the data easier to analyze on a map. I might also decide to reduce it even further since after all that I still have about 60K trees in hand.

The columns. What do they mean?

Currently, for my prototypes I used these columns:

  1. qCaretaker: The entity in charge of taking care of the tree (Department of Public Works, private, etc...).
  2. PlantDate:The date the tree was planted, precise to the hour of day in some cases.
  3. qSpecies:A surprisingly precise field about each trees species. I really need help grouping together similar species because there are SO MANY.
  4. Latitude:The latitude of the trees location.
  5. Longitude:The longitude of the trees location.
